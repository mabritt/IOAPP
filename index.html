Professional Groups

Professional groups are great for networking and keeping up with the latest news within the field. Many have added bonuses such as subscriptions or invitations to conferences. Some require annual fees, however fees are different for students, professionals, etc.

APA

The American Psychological Association is the largest and most well known group within the psychology field. Their mission statement is "to advance the creation, communcation, and application of psychological knowledge to benefit society and improve people's lives."

Benefits of membership:
Subscriptions to Monitor on Psychology and The American Psychologist
Access to online databases
Up to 60% off journal subscriptions
Up to 30% off videos
Discounts on most books
Discount on APA Anual Convention
and many more professional and personal benefits

Fees differ for students and professionals
