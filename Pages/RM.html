Many psychologists joke that a big benefit of studying psychology is the lack of math. However,research methods is an integral part of psychology and requires a basic knowledge of statistics. Think of all the interesting experiments you’ve heard of. Does Pavlov ring a bell? All the work that psychologists do to analyze the data and crunch the numbers is based on research methods. So if you’ve taken a class like this or plan to in the future, know that it is an essential part to your foundation of psych knowledge.
